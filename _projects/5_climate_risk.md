As a research assistant at **City University of Hong Kong**, I applied the ISI-MIP ATTRICI framework (Python, xarray, netCDF4, PyMC/SciPy) to generate counterfactual climate datasets for aviation emission trade-off analysis. I ran the 8-step detrending and quantile-mapping pipeline, then built probabilistic risk-analysis and visualization workflows comparing CO₂ vs. non-CO₂ emission trade-offs. A low-cost experiment reversing the causal direction between the two directly informed the Bayesian prior specification.

**Stack:** Python · xarray · PyMC · SciPy
