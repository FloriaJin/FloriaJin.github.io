Reproduction of **MERINA** (ACM MM '22), a meta-reinforcement-learning approach to adaptive bitrate (ABR) video streaming. Beyond reproducing the method, this repository adds CPU-compatibility fixes, a reproducible adaptation procedure, and a cross-dataset plus Puffer adaptation analysis examining how well the learned controller generalizes to unseen network conditions.

Original implementation: [confiwent/merina](https://github.com/confiwent/merina).

**Stack:** Python · PyTorch · Reinforcement Learning
